The Art of Tea

Discover the distinct characters of our harvest.

Green Tea

Unoxidized and fresh, preserving the natural antioxidants and delicate grassy notes.

Black Tea

Fully oxidized, rich and robust. The classic morning cup with deep malty flavors.

White Tea

Minimal processing, made from young buds. Subtle, sweet, and floral elegance.

Oolong Tea

Semi-oxidized, offering a complex profile somewhere between green and black tea.

Behind the Scenes

Our factory combines century-old traditions with modern precision. From withering the fresh leaves to the final rolling and drying, every step is monitored to ensure perfection.

  • Plucking: Only two leaves and a bud.
  • Withering: Removing moisture naturally.
  • Rolling: Twisting the leaves to release flavor.
  • Drying: Firing to lock in the aroma.
